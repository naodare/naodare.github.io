about

learn more about nao

I am not a minimalist. I strive to create designs that effectively communicate their message while also being aesthetically pleasing.

I graduated from the Communication Design Essentials program at Emily Carr University of Art and Design and British Columbia Institute of Technology.

Growing up, I spent a lot of my time in downtown Vancouver, surrounded by the signage, advertisements, people, and art of the city. All of this influences the design choices I make today.

"Complexity is not the problem, ambiguity is. Simplicity does not solve ambiguity, clarity does."
