BRAND DESIGN
BROWNS SHOES

Brand identity redesign for Canadian shoe retailer, Browns Shoes. The rebrand positioned Browns as “the anti-brand” — emphasizing the quality and personality of the footwear above the brand name.

EVCC (WIP)
Brand identity redesign for East Vancouver Community Centre. The essence of the brand is best captured by it’s “why” — To connect East Van. Every aspect of the identity, from positioning to visuals, is crafted to fulfill that purpose.

WHISKERS
Complete brand identity for pet health monitoring collar brand, Whiskers. The world of tech and healthcare often times feels cold, so I positioned empathy and compassion at the forefront.

MUNCH-KIN
Brand identity for family dental office Munch-kin. Creating a visual language that conveyed professionalism to parents while still being exciting for children was the primary design challenge on this project.
