Brand Design

  • Browns Shoes

    Visual identity redesign for Canadian shoe retailer, Browns Shoes. The redesign positioned Browns as “the anti-brand” — emphasizing the quality and personality of the footwear above the brand name.

  • Whiskers

    Complete brand identity for pet health monitoring collar brand, Whiskers. The world of tech and healthcare often times feels cold, so I positioned empathy and compassion at the forefront.

  • Munch-kin

    Brand identity for family dental office Munch-kin. Creating a visual language that conveyed professionalism to parents while still being exciting for children was the primary design challenge on this project.

  • East Vancouver Community Centre

    Brand identity for East Vancouver Community Centre. The essence of the brand is best captured by it’s “why” — To connect East Van. Every aspect of the identity, from positioning to visuals, is crafted to fulfill that purpose.
